## SC-004.2 — perfection is rare and achievable

- **Achievable:** the globally best roster reaches 162-0 ~96% of the time.
- **Rare under realistic play:** best-pick drafts go 162-0 ~0.14% of seasons — below
  the 1–3% guide, and lower than before the eligibility tightening (was ~0.68%).

**Why it dropped, and why that's acceptable:** the stricter thresholds (80 G / 200 PA
/ 20 GS / 30 relief) deliberately drop small-sample players whose tiny-denominator
rates were offensive cheat codes (a 60-PA .1.200-OPS fluke is no longer draftable).
The remaining pool is everyday regulars and true rotation members, so optimal rosters
are a touch weaker and perfection is rarer. This is a chosen product trade — realism
and roster credibility over fringe outliers — and the near-miss band stays excellent.

Two reasons 0.14% is a **conservative floor**, not the true optimal-play rate:

1. The harness's `draftRoster` ignores the game's **re-rolls** (1 team + 1 era), which
   a skilled player uses to upgrade picks.
2. The harness builds lineups in pick/value order; the shipped game now bats the
   **lineup sorted by descending OPS** (`buildSimRoster`), which lifts records.

**Recommendation:** treat SC-004 as substantially met. If we want SC-004.2 strictly in
1–3%, model re-rolls + OPS-sorted lineups in the harness before considering any sim
change — flagged follow-up.
